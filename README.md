# Project: ODER

**Project: ODER** is a 3D survival horror prototype developed in Unity.

You wake up in the depths of the ODER subterranean research facility. The power is dead, automated security units are roaming the dark corridors with erratic behavior, and biometric systems are failing.

Armed only with a maintenance bat and whatever grid-power supplies you can restore, you must navigate through locked sectors, bypass high-voltage security overrides, and reach the Central AI Core.

But as you dig deeper into the facility's archives, a chilling question arises:

**Did you come here to fix this place... or did it call you back?**
